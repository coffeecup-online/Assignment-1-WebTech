1984

Our website

This is our website about the popular novel 1984. On this page you will find a short video summary about the novel. Also on the other pages we delve into the famous author of the novel George Orwell, the plot of the book and the characters in it. 1984 explores themes such as totalitarianism, propaganda, censorship, and poverty and inequality. The book was published in 1949 however even today these topics remain or maybe have become even more important in our society. For anyone who is looking for a thought provoking thriller or is trying to widen their perspectives on these topics we certainly recomend this read!
